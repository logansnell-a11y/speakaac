4. The private safety channel — important limitations

The private safety channel is not a substitute for emergency services.

If you or a child is in immediate danger, call 911 first. Do not rely solely on the safety channel in a crisis.

The private safety channel sends an email notification to a trusted contact you configure. It depends on several factors outside our control: internet connectivity, the availability of our email delivery provider (EmailJS), whether the trusted contact checks their email promptly, and the accuracy of the contact information you entered.

Speak makes no guarantee that safety alerts will be delivered or acted upon within any specific timeframe. The safety channel is a supplement to — not a replacement for — direct emergency services, 911, child protective services, or other professional crisis resources.

We are not liable for any harm that results from a failure of the safety channel to deliver an alert or from a trusted contact's failure to respond.
