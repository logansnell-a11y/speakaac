Getting started — SLP checklist

From first open to a configured client profile in under 5 minutes.

1

Open the app — no download required

Visit speakaac.org/app.html on any browser. Bookmark it or tap "Add to Home Screen" for a native-app feel on iPad or Android.

2

Create a provider account

Tap Sign In → Create Account. One account manages all your client profiles. Your account is what the Clinic or Institution plan is attached to.

3

Run the setup wizard for your first client

Tap Provider Settings → Run Setup Again. The 10-step wizard asks about diagnosis, age range, motor ability, sensory profile, and preferred vocabulary level. Speak configures the symbol grid automatically.

4

Configure the safety contact

In Provider Settings → Trusted Safety Contact, enter the name and email of the trusted adult (parent, other clinician, supervisor). This is the person notified if the client activates the private help channel.

5

Enable kiosk mode for session use

In Provider Settings → Kiosk Mode, lock the app to fullscreen. The client cannot navigate away. Exit requires your 4-digit Provider PIN.

6

Review session data from the dashboard

After each session, tap Log to see the full timestamped communication history. The Provider Dashboard shows weekly activity charts, most-used symbols, and category breakdown. Export to CSV for documentation.

The safety channel — clinical context

Children with communication disabilities are abused at 3–4 times the rate of their non-disabled peers, and the traditional reporting pipeline breaks down entirely when a child cannot speak. Speak's private safety channel gives every client a mechanism to silently alert a trusted adult outside the primary caretaker relationship — with no visible action on the device and no sound.

In a clinical setting, you can configure the trusted contact as a supervising clinician, a school social worker, or another trusted professional. The alert fires immediately via email and includes the reason the client selected and a timestamp.

The safety channel is free on every plan, forever. It is never paywalled, regardless of subscription tier.

Important: The private safety channel notifies a trusted contact by email. It is a supplement to — not a replacement for — emergency services. In an immediate emergency, call 911. See our Terms of Service for full details.

HIPAA Business Associate Agreement

If you are a healthcare provider or clinic using Speak to support clients and you bill insurance, you may require a HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA). Speak is HIPAA-capable on Clinic and Institution plans — your client data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+) via Supabase, which is SOC 2 Type II certified and supports HIPAA.

To receive a signed BAA: email support@speakaac.org with the subject line "BAA Request" and your clinic or practice name. We'll return a signed agreement within one business day.

For FERPA agreements (school districts), use subject line "FERPA Request".

Our clinical philosophy

Speak is built on the belief that AAC is a complete, valid, permanent communication system — not a stepping stone toward verbal speech. We do not frame nonverbal communication as a deficit to be corrected. The app is designed to give clients more power to communicate in the way that works for them, with no implicit pressure toward a "better" outcome.

If you have questions about clinical fit, symbol vocabulary alignment, or how Speak maps to LAMP (Language Acquisition through Motor Planning) or core vocabulary approaches, email support@speakaac.org. We're happy to talk through implementation.
