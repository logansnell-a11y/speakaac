What Speak does

Built around your child — not around everyone else's

Every existing AAC app gives every child the same grid. Speak configures itself based on who your child actually is.

Onboarding
Personalized from day one
Answer 10 questions about your child — diagnosis, age, motor ability, sensory needs, interests. Speak configures the layout, vocabulary, symbol size, and starting categories automatically. Setup that used to take hours takes minutes.
AI
AI Sentence Builder
Tap a few symbols and Speak uses Claude AI to turn them into a full, natural sentence — spoken aloud. "Want water more" becomes "I would really like some more water, please." Every thought, fully expressed.
Audio
Real recorded audio
Every symbol has a real recorded voice — not robotic text-to-speech. Type anything and it speaks it aloud. Works offline. Works without an internet connection for all core symbols.
Dashboard
Provider dashboard
See what your child communicated today, this week, over time. Most-used symbols. Safety alert log. Category breakdown. A picture of their communication you couldn't see before.
Security
Kiosk mode
Lock the app in fullscreen so the child can't exit or access other apps. Requires a provider PIN to unlock. Built for school tablets and clinic devices.
Standard
ARASAAC pictograms
Every symbol uses ARASAAC — the international AAC pictogram standard used by SLPs and schools worldwide. Clear, familiar, and accessible to every child who has used AAC before.

Common questions

Everything you need to know

Once loaded, all core symbols, audio, and app features work completely offline. An internet connection is only needed to load ARASAAC pictogram images for the first time, and to use the AI Sentence Builder. After your first session, the app is fully cached.
Speak works on any modern browser — iPad, iPhone, Android tablets and phones, Windows, Mac, and Chromebook. You can install it on your home screen from Safari or Chrome like a native app. No App Store, no Google Play, no download fees.
When the child presses Help and chooses "I feel unsafe," Speak silently sends an email to the trusted contact you set up during onboarding — a grandparent, therapist, or teacher. Nothing visible happens on the device. No sound. No mail app opens. The person in the room sees nothing. The trusted contact gets an email within seconds with what the child selected and a timestamp.
Yes. Speak is COPPA-compliant for children under 13 — we don't collect personal data from children, and all profile data is encrypted in transit and at rest. The Clinic and Institution plans include HIPAA compliance for clinical settings. We never sell data or build advertising profiles.
