🗣️ Speak
A voice for every child

Speak is a free AAC (augmentative and alternative communication) app built for nonverbal and minimally verbal children and adults. It gives them symbols, audio, and AI-assisted sentences to communicate — and a private safety channel to report abuse to a trusted adult without anyone in the room knowing.

Made by Logan Snell, an 18-year-old developer in Kansas. Built in the open, for families first.
